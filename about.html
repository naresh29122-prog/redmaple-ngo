Our Mission & Vision

Inspired by Narayan Seva

Red Maple Foundation is dedicated to the philosophy of "Service to Man is Service to God." We aim to provide a bridge between modern technology and traditional grassroots charity.

We believe in organic growth for society, ensuring no child goes hungry and no senior is left behind.
